  • What's your favorite episode?
    • Season 3's The Rabbit and the Turtle. I identify greatly with the main character. That and Episode 6 of the short-lived live action series.
  • What's your favorite season?
    • I'd have to say season 3, because the show really ramps up on the twist endings and grey areas. Going back and watching season 1 after becoming accustomed to season 2 and 3 was such a disappointment.
  • How do you feel about the Hell Girl manga?
    • Never got around to reading it.
  • How do you feel about the live action series?
    • It seemed rushed. I mean, you can't expect much from it, but it sort of started off using plot devices from the anime but never really saw them to their conclusion. This bugged me. Other than that, it was alright. There were a couple of gems and I was surprised how well the series lended itself to live action.
