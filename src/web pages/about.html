About

Hell Girl, also known as Jigoku Shojo, is an anime series produced by Aniplex and Studio Deen. It focuses on the existence of a supernatural system that allows people to take revenge by having other people sent to Hell via the services of the mysterious titular character and her assistants who implement this system. Revenge, injustice, hatred, and the nature of human emotions are common themes throughout the series. -Wikipedia

Hell Girl is an anthology show in the vain of The Outer Limits, meaning that each episode focuses on a different story with a different set of people. There are many different types of episodes to choose from, which are not easily identified by title. The purpose of this website is to provide a way for fans of the series to easily pick out what episodes they want to watch based on category and/or season and also allow fans to search for shows based on titles.
